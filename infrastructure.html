Energy & Power

Empowering Africa with advanced power generation, grid automation, and sustainable energy solutions. Delivering reliable, efficient, and future-ready energy infrastructure.

What We Do in Energy

Power Plant EPC

Turnkey engineering, procurement, and construction for thermal, hydro, and renewable power plants. From feasibility to commissioning, we deliver robust energy infrastructure.

Grid Automation

Smart grid solutions, SCADA integration, and real-time monitoring for efficient, reliable power distribution and management.

Renewable Integration

Solar, wind, and hybrid systems design and deployment. Enabling clean energy transitions for African industries and communities.

Energy Storage & Microgrids

Advanced battery storage, microgrid design, and off-grid power solutions for resilience and sustainability.

Our Products & Equipment

Gas Turbine Generators

High-efficiency turbines for large-scale power generation. Remote monitoring and predictive maintenance included.

  • Output: 50-300 MW
  • Remote diagnostics
  • Automated controls
  • Low emissions

Solar Inverters

Grid-tied and off-grid inverters for solar PV systems. High conversion efficiency and smart monitoring.

  • Efficiency: 98.5%
  • Smart grid ready
  • App monitoring
  • Surge protection

Medium Voltage Switchgear

Reliable switchgear for safe, efficient power distribution. Modular, scalable, and IoT-enabled.

  • Voltage: 11-33kV
  • Modular design
  • IoT monitoring
  • Arc protection

Battery Storage Systems

Lithium-ion and flow battery solutions for grid and off-grid storage. Scalable, safe, and long-lasting.

  • Capacity: 100kWh–10MWh
  • Safety certified
  • Fast charge/discharge
  • Eco-friendly

SCADA & Automation

Supervisory control and data acquisition for real-time grid and plant management. Secure, scalable, and cloud-ready.

  • Cloud integration
  • Cybersecurity
  • Real-time analytics
  • Automated controls

Hybrid Microgrid Controllers

Controllers for integrating solar, wind, diesel, and storage. Seamless, reliable, and smart microgrid management.

  • Multi-source integration
  • Smart load balancing
  • Blackout protection
  • Remote control
